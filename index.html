Casual gaming reimagined.

Who said casual gaming is only for fun? Powered by our unique blockchain technology, we bring you $QUIZ and quiz.com where playing fun trivia games is matched with prizes previously only seen on television.

Our game is easy-to-understand, very engaging, and has a wide appeal across all age groups around the world. It will bring new audiences into blockchain gaming as players will buy, and be rewarded with, our $QUIZ-token.

Our promise

Self Funding

32% of all minted tokens and 90% of our gameplay-fee will be used to fund our prize pools.

Self Regulated

Our smart contracts are designed to function as an automated arbiter, ensuring fair play, guaranteed winners and a guaranteed minimum prize.

Self Executing

All competitions are executed automatically with no possibility for human interference. Winner selection and prize distribution are programmed in our smart contracts.

Transparent Ecosystem

The unique nature of our blockchain architecture creates an open casual gaming system where anyone can verify all transactions on the blockchain.

Connecting worlds

Blockchain technology

blockchain technology is driving a new wave of innovation across industries, including the gaming industry. Unfortunately most of the blockchain games are complex and difficult to master for most people.

Casual gaming

Casual and trivia games are easy fun games that target a very broad audience. Their main attribute is being fun and easy to play without a steep learning curve. What’s lacking is a business model that rewards players.

Winning prizes

We're used to TV shows and esport tournaments handing out significant prizes, but these quizzes and competitions are only open for a selected few. For most people the ability to participate in a competition and win a big prize is beyond their reach.
